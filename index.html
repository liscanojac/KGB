Our committe

We give USSR leaders the intelligence they need to keep our country strong.

As the world's premier foreign intelligence agency, the work we do at KGB is vital to USSR national security. We collect and analyze foreign intelligence and conduct covert action. The Kremlin, including the USSR Soviet Party, make policy decisions informed by the information we provide.

Our Legacy

KGB's legacy is one of brave individuals dedicated to the people, fulfilling our intelligence mission with ingenuity and grit.

Protecting our country is at the heart of our legacy -past, present, and future. From our Soviet roots through today, we carry on the traditions of ingenuity, bravery, and quite sacrifice that have made us one of the world's premier intelligence committees.
